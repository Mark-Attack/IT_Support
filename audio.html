    • Audio Not Working

    Potential Issues

    Physical Connection Issues

    Windows Audio Configuration Issues


    Resolution

    Physical Connections

    • If its USB speakers, then ensure that it is properly connected to the USB port
    • Try connecting to another port
    • Some USB speakers also has a power cord and power button
    • Ensure that the power cord is plugged in and the power button is on


    Windows Configurations

    • One the bottom right of the Windows Taskbar, click the speaker icon and ensure that the volume is turned up
    • Right click the speaker icon and select Sounds
    • On the Playback tab, Ensure that the correct playback device is selected


    • Right click the speaker icon again and select Open Sound Settings
    • Ensure that the correct Input and Output settings are applied


    NB. If on a call app (Teams/Zoom), then configure the in-app audio settings as well.
