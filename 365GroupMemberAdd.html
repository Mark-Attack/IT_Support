    • Adding members to a Group in Microsoft 365

    Prerequisites

    Logged into M365 admin


    • Click the Users dropdown
    • Click Show all


    • Teams and Groups will appear in the menu
    • It can be pinned to the menu to avoid having to continuously click the show all button


    • Click the Teams and Groups dropdown
    • Click Active Teams and Groups


    • Locate the group you wish to add a member to and click the name


    • A popup would appear with all of the group's information
    • Click on the Membership tab, then click members to the left
    • Click the Add Members button
    • locate the member(s) you wish to add and check the box next to their name(s)
    • Click Add once completed
