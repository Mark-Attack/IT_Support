    Other PC Issues


    Resolution

    Black Screen and No Power Light

    • Ensure that the monitor's power cable is properly plugged in on both ends
    • Ensure that the power strip is on
    • Ensure that the outlet is working (Try plugging into a different outlet)
    • Press the monitor's power button (Can be easily overlooked)


    Monitor Says No Signal

    • Ensure that the video cable is properly connected on both ends
    • Try testing with a different video cable
    • Ensure that the Input Source matches the cable type used (HDMI, DisplayPort, etc.)
    • Test with a known working monitor to rule out other issues (Bad display card or motherboard)
